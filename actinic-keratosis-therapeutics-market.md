<p><p>The Actinic Keratosis therapeutics market is categorized into three main types: topical medications, photodynamic therapy, and destructive treatments. Topical medications include creams and gels that target lesions directly, promoting skin cell turnover. Photodynamic therapy utilizes light-sensitive agents activated by light to selectively destroy abnormal cells. Destructive treatments encompass various techniques such as cryotherapy and laser therapy, which physically remove or destroy the affected skin tissue. Together, these approaches offer effective options for managing and treating actinic keratosis.</p></p>
