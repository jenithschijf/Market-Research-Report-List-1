<p><p>Active RFID tags are equipped with their own power source and can transmit signals autonomously, allowing for enhanced tracking and locating capabilities. The market can be divided into two main types: transponders and beacons. Transponders continuously communicate with RFID readers, enabling real-time tracking of assets or personnel. Beacons, on the other hand, broadcast signals at regular intervals and are often used for proximity-based applications, such as location-based services or indoor navigation, providing critical data for asset management and monitoring.</p></p>
