<p><p>The ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil) blasting market includes three main types: Powdered Ammonium Oil Explosive, Porous Granular Ammonium Oil Explosive, and Heavy Ammonium Oil Explosive. Powdered ANFO is finely ground, offering high energy and ease of mixing. Porous Granular ANFO features larger, porous particles for better oil absorption and stability. Heavy ANFO contains added aluminum or other materials for increased density and blast power, ideal for tough conditions. Each type caters to specific mining and construction needs, balancing cost, efficiency, and performance.</p></p>
