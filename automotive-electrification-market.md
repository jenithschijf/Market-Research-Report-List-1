<p><p>The automotive electrification market encompasses various vehicle types based on their powertrains. Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles rely solely on traditional fuel engines. Micro-Hybrid Vehicles utilize start-stop systems for better efficiency. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine gasoline engines with electric motors for improved fuel economy. Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) offer larger batteries that can be charged externally, enabling electric-only driving for short distances. Lastly, Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) are fully electric, powered solely by batteries with no internal combustion components.</p></p>
