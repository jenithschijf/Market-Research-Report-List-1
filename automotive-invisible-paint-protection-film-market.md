<p><p>The Automotive Invisible Paint Protection Film market comprises three main types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers affordability and basic protection, making it suitable for budget-conscious consumers. PU (Polyurethane) provides enhanced flexibility and durability, allowing for better adhesion and resistance to yellowing. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) is the premium option, combining the benefits of both PVC and PU, delivering superior protection against scratches, abrasions, and environmental factors, ensuring longer-lasting aesthetics for vehicle surfaces.</p></p>
