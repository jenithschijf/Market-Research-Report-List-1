<p><p>The Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) market can be categorized into Shallow and Medium AUVs based on operational depth and applications. They are often used for environmental monitoring, marine research, and underwater inspections. Medium AUVs, operating between 100 to 2000 meters, are suitable for deeper explorations, hydrographic surveys, and resource mapping, making them vital for advanced research and industrial applications.</p></p>
