<p><strong>The Battery Management System for Electric Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized BMS</li><li>Distributed BMS</li><li>Modular BMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Management System (BMS) for electric vehicles (EVs) is categorized into three primary types: Centralized BMS, Distributed BMS, and Modular BMS. Centralized BMS manages battery packs from a single control unit, simplifying design but potentially creating a single point of failure. Distributed BMS distributes control across multiple units, enhancing reliability and scalability. Modular BMS combines features of centralized and distributed systems, allowing for flexibility and easy integration of additional modules, making it suitable for various EV applications.</p></p>
