<p><p>A biofilm cleaner is a specialized cleaning agent designed to remove biofilms, which are complex communities of microorganisms that adhere to surfaces and form a protective matrix. These biofilms are resistant to conventional cleaning methods and can cause contamination, infections, and equipment damage in industries such as healthcare, food processing, water treatment, and manufacturing. Biofilm cleaners typically contain enzymes, surfactants, or antimicrobial agents that break down the biofilm matrix and eliminate the embedded microorganisms.</p><p>The Biofilm Cleaner Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of biofilm-related issues and the need for effective cleaning solutions across various industries.
