<p><ul><li>Biaxial Geogrids</li><li>Uniaxial Geogrids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber geogrid market is primarily segmented into two types: biaxial geogrids and uniaxial geogrids. Biaxial geogrids are designed to provide reinforcement in both horizontal and vertical directions, making them ideal for applications like pavement stabilization and soil erosion control. Uniaxial geogrids, on the other hand, are oriented in one direction, offering greater strength and stiffness for applications such as retaining walls or slope stabilization. Both types enhance structural integrity and longevity in various civil engineering projects.</p></p>
