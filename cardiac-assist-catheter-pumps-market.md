<p><p>The Cardiac Assist Catheter Pumps market is primarily divided into two types: Left Ventricular Assisted Catheter Pumps (LVACPs) and Right Ventricular Assisted Catheter Pumps (RVACPs). LVACPs support the left ventricle, enhancing blood flow to the body and improving overall cardiac output, often used in patients with heart failure. RVACPs assist the right ventricle, essential for patients with right-sided heart failure, ensuring adequate blood flow to the lungs. Both types play a crucial role in managing various cardiac conditions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
