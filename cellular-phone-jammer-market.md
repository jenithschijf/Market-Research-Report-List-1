<p><p>The cellular phone jammer market comprises two primary types: stationary signal jammers and portable signal jammers. Stationary signal jammers are designed for fixed locations, such as buildings or secure areas, providing long-term, continuous disruption of cellular signals. They are typically more powerful and cover larger areas. In contrast, portable signal jammers are compact and designed for mobility, allowing users to disrupt signals on the go.
