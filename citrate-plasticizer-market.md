<p><p>The citrate plasticizer market comprises various types, including tributyl citrate (TBC), acetyl tributyl citrate (ATBC), and triethyl citrate (TEC). TBC is commonly used for polyvinyl chloride (PVC) due to its effective flexibility and low volatility. ATBC, a non-toxic alternative, is favored in food packaging and medical applications. TEC serves as a versatile option in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. Other citrate plasticizers encompass different formulations tailored for specific industries, enhancing material flexibility and performance while promoting eco-friendliness.</p></p>
