Fuel type heaters often use wood or other combustible materials, providing a rustic ambiance while being eco-friendly. Electric heaters are easy to use and maintain, delivering consistent heat with lower emissions, suitable for areas where gas supply is limited. Each type caters to varying business needs and outdoor settings.</p></p>
