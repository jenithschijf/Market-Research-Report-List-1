<p><p>The decoration terrazzo flooring market comprises two primary types: inorganic terrazzo and epoxy terrazzo. Inorganic terrazzo is made from cement and aggregates, known for its durability and traditional aesthetic, making it suitable for various commercial and residential applications. In contrast, epoxy terrazzo utilizes a polymer-based resin, offering increased flexibility in design, ease of installation, and resistance to moisture and stains. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, contributing to their popularity in contemporary flooring solutions.</p></p>
