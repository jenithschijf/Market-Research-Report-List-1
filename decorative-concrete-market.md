<p><p>The decorative concrete market offers various types to enhance aesthetics and functionality. Exposed concrete showcases aggregate on the surface, creating a textured finish. Stamped concrete mimics materials like stone or tile through patterns. Polished concrete delivers a sleek, reflective surface achieved by grinding. Engraved concrete involves carving designs into the surface for a personalized touch. Stained concrete applies color treatments to enrich or alter the appearance. Each type caters to diverse applications in residential and commercial spaces, enhancing visual appeal and durability.</p></p>
