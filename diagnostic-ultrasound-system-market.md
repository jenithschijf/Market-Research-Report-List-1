<p><p>The diagnostic ultrasound system market is primarily divided into two main types: Color Doppler ultrasound imaging systems and Black & White ultrasound imaging systems. Color Doppler systems enhance visualization of blood flow and can identify vascular conditions, making them crucial for cardiology and obstetrics. In contrast, Black & White ultrasound systems provide basic imaging using grayscale, commonly used for initial assessments in various medical fields. Both types offer vital diagnostic capabilities, catering to different clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
