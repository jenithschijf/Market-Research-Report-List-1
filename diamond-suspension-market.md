<p><p>Diamond suspension is a liquid medium containing fine diamond particles used for polishing, lapping, and precision grinding. The market is segmented into oil-based and water-based suspensions. Oil-based suspensions offer superior lubrication, reduced evaporation, and are ideal for hard materials, providing a smoother finish. Water-based suspensions are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and suitable for softer materials, offering faster cutting rates. Both types cater to industries like electronics, optics, and automotive, with choice depending on material compatibility and environmental considerations.</p></p>
