<p><strong>The Engine Exhaust System Muffler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Exhaust Muffler</li><li>Reactive Exhaust Muffler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine exhaust system muffler market consists primarily of two types: absorption exhaust mufflers and reactive exhaust mufflers. Absorption mufflers utilize sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise levels by dissipating sound energy as it passes through, resulting in a quieter operation. In contrast, reactive mufflers employ internal chambers and baffles to create opposing sound waves that cancel out noise through constructive and destructive interference. Both types are essential for meeting emissions regulations and enhancing the performance of vehicles.</p></p>
