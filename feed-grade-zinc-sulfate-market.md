<p><p>The feed grade zinc sulfate market primarily consists of two types: zinc sulfate monohydrate and zinc sulfate heptahydrate. Zinc sulfate monohydrate contains one molecule of water and is often preferred for its higher zinc concentration, which supports animal health effectively. Zinc sulfate heptahydrate, with seven water molecules, is typically more cost-effective and offers bulk applications. Both forms are essential in animal nutrition, improving growth rates, immune function, and overall livestock productivity in the agriculture sector.</p></p>
