<p><p>The Filtration Sterilization Equipment market encompasses various technologies used to remove contaminants from liquids and gases. Deep filters use a thick matrix to capture particles through depth filtration, suitable for larger volumes and higher loads. In contrast, membrane filters utilize a porous barrier that selectively allows certain sizes of particles and microorganisms to pass, ensuring high sterility. Both types play crucial roles in industries like pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food processing, ensuring product safety and compliance with health standards.</p></p>
