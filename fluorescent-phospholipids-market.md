<p><ul><li>Solvent</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorescent phospholipids market includes two primary types: solvent-based and powder-based. Solvent-based fluorescent phospholipids are dissolved in liquid carriers, making them ideal for applications requiring precise delivery and easy integration into aqueous systems, such as cell membrane studies and drug delivery. Powder-based fluorescent phospholipids are in dry form, offering stability, longer shelf life, and versatility for reconstitution in various solvents. Both types are widely used in research, diagnostics, and biotechnological applications for labeling and imaging purposes.</p></p>
