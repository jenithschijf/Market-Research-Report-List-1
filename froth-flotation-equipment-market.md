<p><p>Froth flotation equipment is a specialized machinery used in mineral processing to separate valuable minerals from ore by exploiting differences in their surface properties. The process involves creating a froth layer on the surface of a slurry, where hydrophobic particles attach to air bubbles and rise to the top, while hydrophilic particles remain in the slurry. This equipment is widely used in industries such as mining, wastewater treatment, and recycling.</p><p>The Froth Flotation Equipment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
