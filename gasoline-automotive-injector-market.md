<p><strong>The Gasoline Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Port Fuel Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gasoline automotive injector market is primarily segmented into two types: Port Fuel Injection (PFI) and Direct Injection (DI). PFI systems spray fuel into the intake manifold, allowing for better mixing with air before entering the combustion chamber, which enhances throttle response and reduces emissions. In contrast, DI systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber under high pressure, improving fuel efficiency and power output, while also enabling more precise control of the combustion process for performance optimization.</p></p>
