<p><p>Hemodialysis water treatment plants utilize various technologies to ensure safe, purified water for dialysis procedures. Carbon filtration removes chlorine and organic contaminants, while reverse osmosis reduces dissolved impurities. Ultrafiltration eliminates larger particles and bacteria. Deionized water systems further purify by removing ions. Ultraviolet TOC reduction minimizes total organic carbon, and ultraviolet disinfection systems inactivate pathogens. The single patient (acute) treatments market focuses on portable, efficient solutions designed for individual patient use, enhancing accessibility and treatment quality in critical care scenarios.</p></p>
