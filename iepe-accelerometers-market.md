<p><ul><li>Singal Axis</li><li>Triaxial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IEPE (Integrated Electronic Piezoelectric) accelerometers are categorized into two main types based on their measurement capabilities: single-axis and triaxial. Single-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along one specific direction, making them ideal for applications requiring focused data, such as monitoring vibrations in a specific axis. Triaxial accelerometers, on the other hand, measure acceleration along three orthogonal axes simultaneously, providing comprehensive motion analysis. This versatility makes triaxial sensors suitable for complex applications, including structural health monitoring and dynamic behavior assessment in various industries.</p></p>
