<p><ul><li>Bottom Vivration</li><li>Side Viration</li><li>Top Viration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immersible ultrasonic transducers are classified into three market types based on their placement and vibration direction: bottom vibration, side vibration, and top vibration. Bottom vibration transducers emit sound waves from the bottom, optimizing energy transfer in applications like baths or tanks. Side vibration transducers generate sound waves from the side, ideal for processing large volumes of fluid or solids. Top vibration transducers produce waves from above, often used in applications requiring surface agitation or cleaning, ensuring versatile implementation across various industries.</p></p>
