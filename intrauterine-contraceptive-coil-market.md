<p><strong>The Intrauterine Contraceptive Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hormonal Intrauterine Device</li><li>Copper Intrauterine Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intrauterine contraceptive coil market comprises two primary types: hormonal intrauterine devices (IUDs) and copper intrauterine devices. Hormonal IUDs release progestin to prevent ovulation, thicken cervical mucus, and thin the uterine lining, providing effective and long-term contraception. Copper IUDs use copper's spermicidal properties to inhibit sperm movement and fertilization without hormones, offering an alternative for those seeking non-hormonal options. Both devices are highly effective, long-lasting, and reversible methods of contraception, catering to diverse reproductive health needs.</p></p>
