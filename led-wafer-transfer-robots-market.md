<p><p>The LED wafer transfer robots market is categorized into two primary types: atmospheric wafer transfer robots and vacuum wafer transfer robots. Atmospheric wafer transfer robots operate in standard ambient conditions, suitable for applications where cleanliness and minimal contamination are manageable. In contrast, vacuum wafer transfer robots function in controlled vacuum environments, essential for sensitive processes that require stringent contamination control. Both types play crucial roles in the semiconductor and LED manufacturing industries, ensuring precise handling and transfer of delicate wafers.</p></p>
