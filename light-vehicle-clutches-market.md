<p><p>The light vehicle clutches market comprises several types, including electromagnetic clutches, friction clutches, and others. Electromagnetic clutches use an electromagnet to engage and disengage the clutch, providing precise control. Friction clutches rely on friction between surfaces to transmit power, offering reliability and performance for standard applications. Other types may include hydraulic or cable-operated clutches that cater to specific vehicle needs. Each type serves unique functions within light vehicles, impacting efficiency, drivability, and overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
