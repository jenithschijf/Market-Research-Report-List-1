<p><p>The liquid epoxy resins market is categorized into low viscosity and high viscosity types. Low viscosity liquid epoxy resins are ideal for applications requiring excellent flow and wetting properties, making them suitable for coatings, adhesives, and composite materials. In contrast, high viscosity liquid epoxy resins offer enhanced structural integrity and are preferred for applications demanding thicker formulations, such as thick coatings and potting compounds. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, including construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
