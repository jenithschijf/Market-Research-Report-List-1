<p><p>The modified silicone market is categorized into reactive and non-reactive silicone fluids. Reactive silicone fluids can chemically bond with substrates, enabling enhanced durability, flexibility, and resistance in applications like coatings and sealants. Non-reactive silicone fluids, on the other hand, provide lubrication, insulation, and anti-foaming properties without forming chemical bonds. These distinctions influence their use across industries such as automotive, construction, and personal care, with each type serving specific functional needs while enhancing product performance.</p></p>
