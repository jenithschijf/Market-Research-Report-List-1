<p><p>The natural gas heavy truck market is primarily divided into two types: LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) heavy trucks and CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) heavy trucks. LNG heavy trucks utilize liquefied natural gas stored at extremely low temperatures, enabling a higher energy density and longer range, making them suitable for long-haul transportation. In contrast, CNG heavy trucks operate on compressed natural gas, which is stored at high pressures, typically used for shorter routes due to their limited range but offering quicker refueling options.</p></p>
