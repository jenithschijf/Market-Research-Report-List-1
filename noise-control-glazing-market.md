<p><p>Noise control glazing includes various types of window systems designed to minimize external noise. Single glazing consists of a single pane of glass, providing limited sound insulation. Double glazing features two panes with an air gap, significantly reducing noise transmission and improving thermal performance. Triple glazing incorporates three panes, offering superior sound insulation and energy efficiency. Each type caters to different needs, balancing factors like cost, performance, and installation requirements, making them suitable for various residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
