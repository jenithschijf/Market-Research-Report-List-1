<p><p>An Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) is an essential instrument used in fiber optic networks to measure the characteristics of optical fibers. It works by sending a series of light pulses down the fiber and analyzing the reflected light to detect faults, measure distance, and evaluate the fiber's overall performance. This technology is critical for network installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting.</p><p>The OTDR market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-speed internet, advancements in telecommunications, and the proliferation of fiber optic networks.
