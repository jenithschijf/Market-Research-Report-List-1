<p><p>The passenger vehicle gasoline engine exhaust valve market comprises three main types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single material, typically providing reliability and cost-effectiveness. Bimetallic valves combine two materials, enhancing performance and resistance to heat. Hollow valves feature a lightweight design, often filled with gases for better thermal expansion management. Each type caters to different engine specifications, optimizing performance, durability, and efficiency in various automotive applications.</p></p>
