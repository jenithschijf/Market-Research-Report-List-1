<p><p>The Penstock Plate market consists of two primary types: Non-rising Spindle and Rising Spindle. Non-rising Spindle valves feature a fixed stem with the disc moving vertically, making them suitable for applications with limited overhead space. In contrast, Rising Spindle valves have a stem that extends upwards as the valve opens, providing visible indications of valve positioning. Both types are crucial in controlling water flow in hydraulic systems, with specific applications based on space constraints and operational visibility requirements.</p></p>
