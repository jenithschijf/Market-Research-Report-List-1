<p><p>The pH sensors market comprises several types, including glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors are traditional and widely used, utilizing a glass electrode to measure pH levels. ISFET sensors, or ion-sensitive field-effect transistors, offer a solid-state alternative with enhanced durability and miniaturization capabilities. Other types may include polymer or optical sensors, each offering specific advantages for various applications. These diverse sensor technologies cater to industries like water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring versatility in pH measurement.</p></p>
