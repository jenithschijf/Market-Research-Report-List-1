<p><p>The pigments market is primarily divided into inorganic and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are derived from mineral sources and are known for their stability, opacity, and durability, making them ideal for applications in coatings, plastics, and construction materials. Organic pigments, on the other hand, are derived from carbon-based compounds, offering vibrant colors and excellent tinting strength. They are widely used in printing inks, textiles, and cosmetics. Both types are essential in various industries, catering to different performance and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
