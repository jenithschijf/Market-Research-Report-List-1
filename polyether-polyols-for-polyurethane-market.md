PO-based polyols are synthetic polyols derived from propylene oxide, known for their versatility and performance in various applications, including flexible foams and coatings. In contrast, NOPs are derived from renewable natural oils, offering an eco-friendly alternative that caters to increasing sustainability demands. Both types serve critical functions in manufacturing diverse polyurethane products across multiple industries.</p></p>
