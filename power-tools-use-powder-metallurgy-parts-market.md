<p><p>The Power Tools Use Powder Metallurgy Parts market is primarily segmented into two types: PM (Powder Metallurgy) and MIM (Metal Injection Molding). PM involves compacting metal powders and sintering them to create strong, lightweight components commonly used in power tools. MIM combines powdered metals with a binder material, allowing for more complex shapes and fine details. Both methods enhance production efficiency and reduce waste, making them ideal for high-performance power tool applications in industries requiring durability and precision.</p></p>
