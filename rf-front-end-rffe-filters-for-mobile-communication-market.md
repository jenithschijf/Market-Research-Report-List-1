<p><ul><li>SAW Filter</li><li>BAW Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Front End (RFFE) filters are essential components in mobile communication, enhancing signal quality and reducing interference. SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) filters utilize acoustic waves to achieve miniaturized, efficient filtering suitable for a range of frequencies, often found in smartphones and other portable devices. In contrast, BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) filters use bulk acoustic waves for improved performance at higher frequencies, offering better insertion loss and selectivity. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-performance, compact components in mobile technology.</p></p>
