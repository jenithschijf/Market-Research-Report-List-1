In contrast, Natural Silk, derived from silkworms, provides a rich, soft texture and boasts superior breathability and durability, making it a premium choice. Both types cater to diverse consumer demands, with Rayon Silk focusing on affordability and style, while Natural Silk emphasizes quality and luxury.</p></p>
