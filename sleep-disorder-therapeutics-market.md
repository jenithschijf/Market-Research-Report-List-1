<p><p>The Sleep Disorder Therapeutics Market encompasses various types of disorders, including insomnia, excessive sleep, and disturbed sleep. Insomnia involves difficulty falling or staying asleep, leading to therapeutic solutions like prescription medications and behavioral therapies. Excessive sleep refers to conditions like hypersomnia, where individuals experience excessive daytime sleepiness, often treated with stimulants. Disturbed sleep includes disruptions in sleep patterns, such as sleep apnea, managed through continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) devices or lifestyle modifications. Each disorder requires tailored therapeutic approaches for effective management.</p></p>
