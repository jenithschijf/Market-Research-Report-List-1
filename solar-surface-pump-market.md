<p><p>The solar surface pump market is primarily divided into two main types: DC and AC pumps. DC pumps operate directly on solar energy, making them efficient for small-scale irrigation or water supply systems, often used in remote locations. In contrast, AC pumps utilize an inverter to convert solar power to alternating current, enabling higher capacity operations, suitable for larger agricultural or industrial applications. Both types are designed to harness renewable energy, promoting sustainability while meeting diverse water needs.</p></p>
