<p><p>The Solvent-based In-Mold Coatings market consists of two primary types: one-component and two-component coatings. One-component in-mold coatings are pre-mixed and ready to use, providing ease of application and faster production times. In contrast, two-component in-mold coatings require a separate hardener to activate, offering enhanced performance and durability. Both types are utilized in various industries for applications such as automotive and consumer goods, providing aesthetic finishes and protection during the molding process.</p></p>
