<p><p>The starter motors market is categorized into three main types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic starter motors. Electric starter motors are widely used in automotive applications for their efficiency and compact design. Pneumatic starter motors utilize compressed air for ignition, often found in heavy machinery and aircraft for their reliability in extreme conditions. Hydraulic starter motors function using hydraulic pressure, commonly used in larger industrial engines due to their robustness and ability to start under heavy load, offering distinct advantages across various applications.</p></p>
