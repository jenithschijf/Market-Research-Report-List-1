<p><p>The tension sensors market features three primary types: Axle Table Type, Through Shaft Type, and Cantilever Type. Axle Table Type sensors are designed to measure tension in rotating axles, suitable for heavy machinery. Through Shaft Type sensors provide precise tension measurement within a shaft, ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. Cantilever Type sensors utilize a beam under load, making them versatile for various industries. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to specific operational requirements in tension monitoring.</p></p>
