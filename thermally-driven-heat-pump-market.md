<p><p>The thermally driven heat pump market encompasses various systems based on their loop configurations. Vertical closed loop systems utilize deep boreholes to circulate fluid for efficient heating and cooling. Horizontal closed loops spread pipes across a large land area, suitable for regions with ample space. Open loop systems draw groundwater directly for heat exchange, ideal in areas with sufficient aquifers. Other market types may include hybrid systems or newer technologies that integrate renewable energy sources to enhance efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
