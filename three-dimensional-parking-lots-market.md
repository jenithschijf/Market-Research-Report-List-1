<p><p>Three-dimensional parking lots are categorized into several market types based on their operational mechanisms. The Lifting and Traversing Class involves systems that lift and move vehicles horizontally. Simple Lift systems elevate cars vertically with minimal complexity. Roadway Stacking allows vehicles to be parked in layers, optimizing space. Plane Movement involves horizontal car movement on a flat plane. Vertical Lift systems focus solely on vertical elevation, while Vertical Loop configurations utilize a looping mechanism to maximize space efficiency, enhancing urban parking solutions.</p></p>
