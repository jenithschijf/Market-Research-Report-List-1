<p><ul><li>Calcium Tungstate Method</li><li>Secondary Ammonium Tungstate Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tungsten Trioxide market is influenced by production methods like the Calcium Tungstate and Secondary Ammonium Tungstate processes. The Calcium Tungstate Method involves extracting tungsten from ores, converting it to calcium tungstate, and then processing it into tungsten trioxide. The Secondary Ammonium Tungstate Method refines tungsten scrap or residues into ammonium tungstate, which is then converted to tungsten trioxide. Both methods cater to diverse industrial applications, with the choice depending on cost, efficiency, and raw material availability.</p></p>
