<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline UV Generator</li><li>Open Channel UV Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) generators market comprises two primary types: inline UV generators and open channel UV generators. Inline UV generators are integrated into pipe systems, directly treating fluids as they flow, making them ideal for applications requiring continuous water purification or disinfection. Open channel UV generators operate in open bodies of water, utilizing a UV lamp setup above the water to disinfect larger volumes effectively. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as wastewater treatment, drinking water purification, and industrial processes.</p></p>
