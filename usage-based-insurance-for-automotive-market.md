<p><strong>The Usage-based Insurance For Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pay-As-You-Drive</li><li>Pay-How-You-Drive</li><li>Manage-How-You-Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Usage-based insurance (UBI) for automotive markets is categorized into three types: Pay-As-You-Drive (PAYD), Pay-How-You-Drive (PHYD), and Manage-How-You-Drive (MHYD). PAYD charges premiums based on the distance driven, rewarding low-mileage drivers. PHYD factors in driving behavior, such as speed and braking habits, offering discounts for safe driving practices. MHYD goes further by providing feedback and tools for drivers to improve their habits, promoting safer driving while adjusting premiums based on behavior changes.</p></p>
