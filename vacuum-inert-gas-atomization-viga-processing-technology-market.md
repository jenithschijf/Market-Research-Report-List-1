<p><p>Vacuum Inert Gas Atomization (VIGA) Processing Technology is a method used to produce fine metallic powders through the rapid solidification of molten metal. In this process, a stream of molten metal is atomized into fine particles using inert gas in a vacuum environment, which prevents oxidation and contamination. VIGA is particularly popular in the production of high-performance alloys and specialty materials required in aerospace, automotive, and medical applications.</p><p>The market for VIGA processing technology is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-quality metal powders in additive manufacturing and various industrial applications.
