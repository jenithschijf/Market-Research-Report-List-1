<p><p>Waste collection trucks come in various types, each designed for specific waste management needs. Front loaders utilize forks to lift bins from the front for efficient loading, while rear loaders are equipped with a compaction system at the back for manual or automated collection. Side loaders operate with side-mounted arms to pick up bins. Pneumatic collection trucks use vacuum systems for lightweight waste, and grapple trucks feature articulated arms for bulk materials. Liquid tankers transport hazardous or liquid waste safely.</p></p>
